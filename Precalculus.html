Spring 2023

Instructor: Boris Tsvelikhovskiy    bdt18 AT pitt.edu

Overview

The goal of the course is to build a proficiency of Algebra and Trigonometry necessary for scientific calculus, Math 0220 or other related science courses. A variety of topics
are studied: functions, rational functions, graphs, asymptotes, inverse functions, logarithmic and exponential functions, conic sections, elementary transformations of graphs,
trigonometric identities and equations, vectors, polar coordinates and complex numbers.
