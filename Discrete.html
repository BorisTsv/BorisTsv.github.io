MATH 11: Introduction to Discrete Structures
Spring 2024

Instructor: Boris Tsvelikhovskiy    borist AT ucr.edu

Overview

The course will provide a comprehensive exploration of fundamental mathematical concepts and their applications. Topics will include an introduction to set theory and counting principles, as well as the method of mathematical induction. We will discuss functions, algorithms, logical operations, and various aspects of combinatorics. The course will culminate with an examination of elementary probability theory.

Textbook

  • The textbook for this course is "Schaum's Outline of Discrete Mathematics", by Lipschutz and Lipson, 3rd Edition.
